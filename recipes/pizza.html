Description

A pizza is a flat and round base of bread with topping of all kinds, sliced into triangliar peices and shared.

Ingredients

  • pizza base
  • tomato sauce
  • ham
  • cheese

Steps

  1. put base down
  2. spread tomato sauce on base evenly
  3. layer ham on top
  4. layer chesse on top
  5. cook at 180 of 25 mins
